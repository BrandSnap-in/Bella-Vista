Our Story

Warmth, craft, and a view worth lingering over.

Bella Vista was created for evenings that deserve a slower rhythm: hand-rolled pasta, flame-finished mains, expressive wines, and the calm confidence of a dining room that knows how to take care of people.

Executive Chef Marco Bellini leads the kitchen with a seasonal Italian lens, pairing local produce with coastal flavors and classic technique. Every plate is built around freshness, balance, and a little theatre at the table.
